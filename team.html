Leadership

Guiding our vision for exceptional design

David Alte

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

With over 15 years of experience in luxury design and craftsmanship, David founded Alte Luxury Line in 2018 with a vision to create bespoke spaces that blend functionality with artistic expression. His background in both architecture and fine woodworking gives him a unique perspective on design challenges. David leads our team with a commitment to quality that has become the hallmark of our brand.

Under his leadership, Alte Luxury Line has grown from a small workshop to a premier design firm serving clients across the region. David's hands-on approach ensures that every project maintains the highest standards of craftsmanship.
